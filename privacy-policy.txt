INSTALILY MEETING NOTES HELPER - PRIVACY POLICY

Last Updated: August 4, 2025

1. INTRODUCTION

Instalily Meeting Notes Helper ("the Extension") is a Chrome extension that connects AI notetaking platforms (Fathom, Circleback) to Instalily's Cosailor platform. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

2. INFORMATION WE COLLECT

2.1 Google Account Information
- Email address
- Name  
- Profile picture
- OAuth access tokens for authentication

2.2 Platform Authentication Data
- Authentication cookies from connected AI notetaking services (Fathom, Circleback)
- Login session information  
- Platform connection status
- Cookie expiration and refresh status

2.3 Usage Information
- Extension settings and preferences
- Connection history and status
- Error logs for troubleshooting

3. HOW WE USE YOUR INFORMATION

3.1 Core Functionality
- Authenticate with your Google account using OAuth 2.0
- Establish secure connections to AI notetaking platforms
- Harvest authentication cookies to maintain platform sessions
- Store connection status and user preferences
- Enable cookie refresh when sessions expire

3.2 Service Improvement
- Monitor connection reliability and success rates
- Troubleshoot authentication and connection issues
- Improve extension functionality and user experience

4. DATA STORAGE AND SECURITY

4.1 Local Storage (Browser)
- OAuth tokens cached locally for session management
- Platform connection status and preferences
- Temporary authentication data during connection process

4.2 Cloud Storage (Google Firestore)
- User profile information (email, name, picture)
- Platform connection status and metadata
- Authentication cookie data for maintaining sessions
- All data encrypted in transit and at rest

4.3 Security Measures
- All data transmission uses HTTPS encryption
- OAuth 2.0 for secure Google authentication
- Access tokens automatically expire and refresh
- No passwords stored by the extension
- Authentication cookies handled securely and not exposed

5. DATA SHARING

5.1 We DO NOT share your data with third parties except:
- Google Firestore (for secure data storage and user authentication)
- The original AI notetaking platforms (only to maintain your authorized sessions)
- Instalily's Cosailor platform (for integration purposes as intended by user)

5.2 We DO NOT:
- Sell your personal information
- Share authentication data with unauthorized parties
- Use your data for advertising purposes
- Access more data than necessary for core functionality
- Share cookies with platforms other than their origin

6. YOUR RIGHTS AND CHOICES

6.1 Access and Control
- Connect/disconnect platforms at any time through the extension
- View connection status and history
- Refresh expired authentication sessions
- Control which platforms are connected

6.2 Data Deletion
- Disconnect platforms to stop data collection
- Uninstalling the extension removes all local data
- Contact us to request cloud data deletion
- Platform connections can be revoked individually

7. COOKIES AND AUTHENTICATION

7.1 We collect authentication cookies from AI notetaking platforms solely for:
- Maintaining authenticated sessions with your consent
- Detecting successful logins and connection status
- Refreshing expired sessions to maintain connectivity
- Enabling seamless integration with Instalily's Cosailor

7.2 We do NOT use cookies for:
- Advertising, marketing, or tracking purposes
- Cross-site tracking or analytics
- Any purpose beyond maintaining authorized platform connections

8. CHILDREN'S PRIVACY

This extension is not intended for users under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

9. USER CONSENT AND CONTROL

9.1 Explicit Consent Required
- Users must explicitly connect each platform through authentication flow
- Clear indication when authentication cookies are being collected
- Users can disconnect platforms at any time
- No background or automatic data collection

9.2 Transparency
- Clear indication of connected vs. disconnected platforms
- Status indicators for cookie expiration and refresh needs
- User-initiated actions for all data collection

10. CHANGES TO THIS POLICY

We may update this privacy policy to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. Updates will be posted with a new "Last Updated" date. Continued use of the extension after updates constitutes acceptance of changes.
